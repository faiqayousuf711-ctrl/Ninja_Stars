Facts About dogs

Dogs evolved from extinct wolves and were the first animals domesticated by humans, known for their incredible sense of smell, unique nose prints, and ability to understand hundreds of words and gestures, much like a two-year-old child.

They sweat from their paw pads, communicate through tail wags, can sense human emotions, and are beneficial for human health.
